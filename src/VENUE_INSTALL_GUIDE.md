## üìÖ Step 1: Choose Your Install Time

### Available Options
When you receive approval, the venue offers **3 install date/time options**:

**Option A: Quick Install (Next Business Day)**
- Fastest turnaround
- Usually within 24-48 hours of approval
- Your artwork goes live immediately
- Best for: Artists who want to start selling ASAP

**Option B: Standard Install (Within 1 Week)**
- Most popular choice
- Gives you time to prepare everything
- Venue has time to prepare the space
- Best for: Most artists (default recommended)

**Option C: Flexible Install (Within 2 Weeks)**
- Maximum flexibility
- Time to coordinate with venue for perfect placement
- Ensures the space is optimally prepared
- Best for: Complex installations or large artwork

### How to Select
1. Log into your Artwalls dashboard
2. Find your approved artwork
3. Click **"Schedule Installation"**
4. Review the 3 venue-provided time slots
5. Select your preferred window
6. **Confirm** - You'll receive an email with:
   - Date & time of your install window
   - Venue contact info
   - Parking/entrance instructions
   - Key contact person at venue

## üé® Step 2: Prepare Your Artwork

### Safety Checklist
- [ ] Artwork is clean and ready for public display
- [ ] Frame/mounting is secure and stable
- [ ] Hardware is appropriate for weight/dimensions
- [ ] No sharp edges or protrusions
- [ ] Artwork won't be damaged by temperature/humidity changes
- [ ] Artist name/title visible (if labeling in person)

### Hardware Requirements

**For Light Artwork (< 5 lbs)**
- Standard picture hanging nails or hooks
- Wall anchors if needed
- Painter's tape for position marking

**For Medium Artwork (5-20 lbs)**
- Heavy-duty picture hooks
- Multiple anchor points
- Secure mounting hardware

**For Heavy Artwork (> 20 lbs)**
- Coordinate with venue for installation
- May need professional hanging service
- Venue may provide hardware

## üè™ Step 3: Install Your Artwork

### Before You Arrive
**Contact the venue the day before** to confirm:
- You're still coming at the scheduled time
- Any special instructions (locked door, parking, etc.)
- Who to ask for when you arrive

### At the Venue

**1. Check In (2 minutes)**
- Introduce yourself to venue staff
- Confirm they're expecting you
- Ask about the designated wall space

**2. Inspect the Space (2 minutes)**
- Verify the wall condition
- Check lighting on the artwork area
- Confirm placement matches approval (e.g., "Main Dining Area")
- Take a photo of the empty space for your records

**3. Install Your Artwork (10-20 minutes)**
- Use appropriate hardware for your artwork type
- Position artwork at eye level when possible (4-5 feet from floor)
- Ensure it's level and secure
- Step back and verify it looks good
- Clean up any debris

**4. Document Installation (2 minutes)**
- Take photos of installed artwork from customer view
- Get a confirmation photo with venue staff (optional)
- Note any details about placement in Artwalls dashboard

## üîó Step 4: Place & Test the QR Code

### Getting Your QR Code

**Your QR code PNG is available:**
- In your Artwalls dashboard under "Approved Listings"
- Click **"Download QR Code"** ‚Üí PNG (1200√ó1200px, high resolution)
- **Print on:** Cardstock or waterproof label stock (glossy recommended)
- **Size:** 4√ó6 inches is standard (prints well at 300 DPI)

### Placement Tips

**Position the QR Code:**
- ‚úÖ **Eye level** (4-5 feet from ground, matching artwork height)
- ‚úÖ **Within 2 feet of the artwork** (so customers know it's associated)
- ‚úÖ **Well-lit area** (avoid shadows, backlighting issues)
- ‚úÖ **Accessible** (customers can easily aim their phone camera)
- ‚úÖ **Protected** (won't be rubbed against or damaged)

**Bad Placements to Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Too low (below waist height - customers miss it)
- ‚ùå Too high (above eye level, hard to read)
- ‚ùå In shadow or dark corner
- ‚ùå Covered by furniture or plants
- ‚ùå More than 3 feet away from artwork
- ‚ùå Placed upside down or sideways

### Mounting Your QR Label

**Option 1: Printed Label (Recommended)**
1. Print QR code on waterproof sticker stock
2. Use corner mounting tape or adhesive backing
3. Position near artwork (preferably at 45¬∞ angle toward customer view)
4. Press firmly to ensure it sticks
5. Test that it's secure

**Option 2: Plastic Sleeve**
1. Insert printed QR in clear plastic sleeve
2. Mount sleeve near artwork with mounting tape
3. Protects from damage and dirt
4. Easy to replace if damaged

**Option 3: Frame-Mounted**
1. Laminate QR code for durability
2. Mount on small stand or frame near artwork
3. Can include "Scan to Purchase" text
4. More professional appearance

### Testing the QR Code (CRITICAL)

**Test immediately after placement:**

1. **Step Back** (6-10 feet away - customer perspective)

2. **Open Camera App** on your smartphone
3. **Point at QR code** - your phone should recognize it automatically
   - You should see a notification to tap
   - Notification should say something like "Scan QR code"

4. **Tap the notification** - it should open a web browser

5. **Verify the Landing Page**
   - ‚úÖ Page loads correctly
   - ‚úÖ Shows your artwork image
   - ‚úÖ Shows correct title and price
   - ‚úÖ Shows **"Buy Now" or "Purchase Artwork"** button
   - ‚úÖ Shows payment breakdown (Artist/Venue/Artwalls split)
   - ‚úÖ Shows venue location information

6. **Test the Purchase Button**
   - Click **"Buy Now"**
   - Page should redirect to Stripe Checkout
   - Checkout should show:
     - Artwork details
     - Price ($X.XX)
     - Payment options (card, Apple Pay, etc.)
   - ‚úÖ This confirms customers can actually buy

7. **Exit Without Purchasing** (unless testing with real payment)
   - Click browser back button or close
   - You're done testing!

### Troubleshooting QR Code Issues

**QR won't scan:**
- [ ] Verify no glare on QR code
- [ ] Move to better lighting
- [ ] Ensure entire QR is visible (not cut off)
- [ ] Check QR isn't upside down
- [ ] Try from different angles

**Scans but page won't load:**
- [ ] Check your internet connection
- [ ] Try again in 30 seconds
- [ ] Contact venue staff about their WiFi
- [ ] If persistent, email support@artwalls.space

**Page loads but wrong artwork shows:**
- [ ] You may have multiple approved artworks
- [ ] Verify artwork title matches
- [ ] Check URL in browser matches your artwork ID
- [ ] Contact support if incorrect

**Can't click "Buy Now" button:**
- [ ] Try different browser (Safari, Chrome)
- [ ] Clear browser cache
- [ ] Try from different device
- [ ] Contact support@artwalls.space

## ‚è∞ During Your Display Period

### Your Responsibilities
- Keep artwork clean and in good condition
- Report any damage to venue immediately (within 48 hours)
- Verify QR code stays in place and isn't damaged
- Monitor sales on your dashboard

### Venue Responsibilities
- Maintain safe, climate-controlled display space
- Protect artwork from damage, theft, loss
- Assist customers if they have questions
- Report any damage within 48 hours

### If Artwork is Damaged
1. **Take photos** of damage
2. **Document details**:
   - When you noticed it
   - What caused it (if known)
   - Type of damage
3. **Contact venue immediately** (within 48 hours)
4. **Report to Artwalls** through your dashboard
5. Follow dispute resolution process

See your **Artist Agreement** for damage policy and liability limits.

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I miss my installation window?**
A: Contact the venue immediately. You'll need to reschedule. Multiple no-shows may result in the approval being cancelled.

**Q: Can the QR code be changed after installation?**
A: No. The QR code is locked to this artwork and venue. If you need a different QR, contact support.

**Q: What if a customer scans and the page shows an error?**
A: This is rare but can happen. Direct them to your social media or ask them to try again in a few minutes. Contact support if it persists.

**Q: Can I move the artwork to a different spot after installation?**
A: Only with venue permission. Some venues are flexible with placement. Ask venue staff.

**Q: Do I need to print the QR code myself?**
A: Recommended for best quality. Venues can print if they're willing. 4√ó6" on cardstock at 300 DPI is ideal.

**Q: What if the artwork doesn't sell?**
A: That's okay! It stays on display for the full duration. You can try a different venue, adjust pricing, or improve marketing.

**Q: How do I handle a damaged artwork claim?**
A: See the "If Artwork is Damaged" section above. Document everything with photos and dates.

**Q: Can I update the price after installation?**
A: No. Price is locked when the venue approves. For next display, choose a new price.
